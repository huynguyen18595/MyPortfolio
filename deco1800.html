Golden Time

Golden Time is a historical learning web platform developed for high school students.

Developed during the Interactive Techonoly course at University of Queensland Aus. My team and I want to attract more youngers to learn Queensland history. Why? History is an essential part of the natonality. If youngers don't have an understanding of their national history, it means they don't lovetheir country enough.

Team:
Steve Nguyen, Shutong Yang, Fenghua Cheng, Sheng Li

Role:
Team Leader, web designer, frontend programmer.

In the beginning, our idea was focusing on creating a map of Queensland. The map would show photos of a specific location at a specific year. The platform was created to give the user more knowledge about the history of Queensland. it was supposed to contain songs, videos, and photos related to history. After doing background research, We set the production target for our project to high school students and undergraduate students who really enjoy history and traditional music.

Section 1.3

Reflection:

In part A, I played the role of a designer and created the basic sketches for the project base on the initial idea of us.
After receiving feedback from the teaching team, we see that there are some problems with our project including :

  • - The idea is not interesting enough to the project's target audiences.
  • - Getting ideas from past projects is not enough.
  • - The target market is not specific enough.
  • - Youtube api doesn't add any value to our project.

Consequently, we kept working on the project idea. we did the background research again. We tested our idea on people article at various ages to find out our target audience.

Section 1.4

After finalizing the outcome of project part A, we decided to keep the same project's concept but change all main functionality of the product(add a game to the product) base on the background research's result. Other team members started drawing some sketches about main functionality. Finally, we created a questionnaire test plan using our low-fidelity prototype (paper prototype) to see how we're going so far.

Section 2.2

Reflection:

After receiving feedback from the teaching team, we see that there are still some issues need to be fixed with our project including:

  • - we was falling a bit behind because of the idea concept.
  • - our project plan was short given.
  • - The target market is still not specific enough.
  • - The idea and functions didn't match each other

Finally, Base on the limitation of api-dataset and the idea of making educational product, we decided to create a educational web-platform including learning process and games to balance between learning and playing as our final idea concept. After doing idea testing on people at various ages and low fidelity prototype testing, we decided that our target audiences are high school students and we started pushing design process. We tried to apply Norman's HCI theory to Ui design. As mentioned, Our project plan was short driven. Consequently, we made a more specific plan from that moment to the end of project part C.
