Golden Time

Golden Time is a historical learning web platform developed for high school students.

Developed during the Interactive Techonoly course at University of Queensland Aus. My team and I want to attract more youngers to learn Queensland history. Why? History is an essential part of the natonality. If youngers don't have an understanding of their national history, it means they don't lovetheir country enough.

In the beginning, our idea was focusing on creating a map of Queensland. The map would show photos of a specific location at a specific year. The platform was created to give the user more knowledge about the history of Queensland. it was supposed to contain songs, videos, and photos related to history. After doing background research, We set the production target for our project to high school students and undergraduate students who really enjoy history and traditional music.

Section 3.5

Design Process

My design process includes 3 states: Choosing main color for product, Logo design, UX design

State1: Choosing the main color product.
The resource of our product mainly come from SQL API dataset containing only black and white photos. As a result, I chose black and white as the product's main colors. Unfortunately, black and white may make people feel bored while using the product so I apply Tonal contrast and Color contrast theory to the design to attract people eyes. Additionally, those two concepts are helpful to control the clarity of wording and information in the interface.

State 2: Logo design.
There are 4 types of logo: Wordmark logo, Lettermark logo, brandmark logo and Iconic logo. In this case, I love Wordmark logo so i decided to draw it by myself using Intuos Draw and photoshop

State 3: UX Design.
when it comes to web and interaction design, Donald Norman's concepts (https://enginess.io/insights/6-principles-design-la-donald-norman) is usually a good start. In this case, for Visibility, every main functional buttons' position are carefully considered. Secondly, for Feedback, there is always a reaction after every user's action. Thirdly, for Consistency, I kept the same reaction for the same type of buttons. Finally, for Mapping, the navigation for user is careful considered, to help user know where they are at while using the product.


Future development

In future development, we have a plan to develop a grading system to record the testing results. The grading data will be connected to the user table in the database so that every student has their own marks. Consequently, It will give a hand to teachers to keep track on student's learning result.
